<objective>
Fix SENT-06: the per-sentence EPUB renderer re-splits para.text with a regex to get display sentences, but para.sentence_translations was built from Punkt+merge chunks. Different counts cause wrong pairings or [TRANSLATION FAILED] entries.

Purpose: Carry the chunk text alongside the translation through the data model so the renderer uses the same segmentation that the translator used.
Output: Paragraph gains sentence_chunk_texts field; translate_sentence() populates it; build_pair_html() uses it; _split_sentences_for_rendering() removed.
</objective>

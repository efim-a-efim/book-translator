## EPUB parser warning — final fix & regression test

**Root cause:** XHTML content in EPUB parsed with BeautifulSoup `lxml` HTML parser → `XMLParsedAsHTMLWarning` at `epub.py:79`.

**Root cause:**
- `_resolve_api_key` had typo: `os.environ.get("OPENAI_API_KEY") or os.environ.get("OPENAI_API_KEY")` (duplicate, never checked `BOOK_TRANSLATOR_API_KEY`)
